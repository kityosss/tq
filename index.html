Fostering Digital Trust

Our platform fosters trust through user verification, eliminating intermediary fraud techniques such as the three-way scam and enhancing individual accountability in the chaos of open markets. Our service helps users interact with verified accounts and provides insights into past interactions and their integrity, empowering more trusted decision-making.

Key Features

Verified Users

Help with identity verification to ensure integrity of account holders.

Interaction Management

Seamlessly manage your interactions with other users through our intuitive interface.

Reviews

Provide and receive reviews to build a strong reputation for your account.

Real-time Updates

Stay informed with instant notifications about your interactions and reviews.

Interactions Overview

These key metrics help users make informed decisions about transactions by providing signs that confirm the legitimacy of the transactions recorded on the account.

Total Interactions

The number of total interactions you've completed on the platform.

Repeated Interactions

Number of users you've interacted with multiple times.

Cross Invites

Mutual invitation occurrences that might signal coordinated behavior patterns.

Reports

Number of times this user has been reported by other users.

Note: This Prototype assumes completed sign-up and verification phases to focus on core functionality. Many features are omitted to maintain fundamental conceptual clarity.
